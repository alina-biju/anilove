Who Are We?

We are students from a Dubai-based Indian School. We love coding and love animals even more. Though Pranamya has a fear of dogs. We wanted to do something for these animals with the resources that we had. On our quest to contribute to the Sustainable Developmental Goals created by the United Nations, we decided 'Anilove' was the best intersection of both. We were first introduced to coding in 8th grade, where we learnt HTML and CSS through CodeAcademy. Since then our love for CS has grown stronger.
